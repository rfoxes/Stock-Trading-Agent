Trump admin export-control order forces Anthropic to disable its top AI models (Fable 5 / Mythos 5) for all foreign users.
Axios / Time / Fortune / Al Jazeera / Nextgov · 2026-06-12 to 06-14
Commerce Sec. Lutnick sent Anthropic CEO Amodei a letter subjecting Fable 5 and Mythos 5 to export controls to any location outside the US and to all foreign persons within it. Anthropic "abruptly disabled" access to the models for ALL customers to comply. Triggered partly by suspicion a China-linked group accessed the new model, and an underlying dispute after Anthropic refused to let the US military use its models for fully-autonomous weapons (Pentagon blacklisted Anthropic). EU pushed back ("should not be discriminatory against partners"). Govt reportedly expects to lift controls once safety concerns are remediated. AI-sector overhang: a reminder that AI-cohort risk is now as much policy/national-security as it is fundamental — relevant to NVDA's China exposure and to AI-software multiples even amid Monday's rally.
