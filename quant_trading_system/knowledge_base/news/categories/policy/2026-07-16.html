USTR imposes 25% Section 301 tariff on certain Brazilian goods (effective July 22)
USTR / CNBC / CNN · 2026-07-16
At Trump's direction, USTR took final Section 301 action imposing a 25% tariff on certain Brazilian goods, effective July 22, after a year-long investigation citing digital-trade & electronic-payment-services measures, preferential tariffs, IP protection, ethanol access, anti-corruption interference, and illegal deforestation. Exemptions: coffee, beef, oranges/OJ, some oil & gas, aerospace parts. A separate forced-labor 301 probe (concluding next week) could add 12.5% → 37.5% total. The "digital trade / electronic payments" citation is a watch item for payments names.
