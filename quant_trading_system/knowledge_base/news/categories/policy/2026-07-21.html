China weighs sweeping AI-model + chip-technology export curbs (affects TSM, QCOM).
Reuters via Benzinga / Pakistan Today · 2026-07-21
Beijing's Ministry of Commerce is holding talks with Alibaba, ByteDance and Z.ai about restricting overseas access to China's most advanced AI models (limiting model-weight downloads and data transfer abroad). Officials are also weighing curbs that would stop foreign chipmakers such as TSMC and Qualcomm from manufacturing advanced semiconductors based on designs from Chinese firms (Huawei, Alibaba, ByteDance). Under consideration, no final decision — a policy watch item for TSM and QCOM.
