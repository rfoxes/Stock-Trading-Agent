GOOGL — Swedish court orders Google to pay ~$1.97B to Klarna's PriceRunner (antitrust); separately, a US judge hands Yelp a procedural win
Bloomberg / Benzinga / PYMNTS · 2026-07-01
Stockholm's Patent and Market Court awarded PriceRunner (Klarna unit) ~$1.97B in damages + interest for Google's preferential treatment of its own comparison-shopping service (built on the 2017 EC Shopping ruling). The court dismissed most of PriceRunner's SEK 80B (~$8.2B) claim, so the award is ~18% of what was sought. Same day, US Magistrate Judge van Keulen barred Google from re-litigating whether it holds monopoly power in general search, aiding Yelp's antitrust suit. Two adverse antitrust developments in one session.
