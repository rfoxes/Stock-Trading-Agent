ADP June private payrolls +98,000 — miss (consensus ~110k; down from 122k in May)
CNBC / Fox Business / Yahoo · 2026-07-01
Private-sector hiring slowed to +98k, below the ~110k consensus and May's +122k. Nearly half the gain (48k) came from education & health services; natural resources/mining was the only sector in the red (−5k). Annual pay held at 4.4% for job-stayers, 6.6% for switchers. ADP's chief economist cited "a slowdown in job creation" amid both softer demand and labor-supply constraints. A soft first labor read of jobs week into the higher-for-longer framework.
ISM Manufacturing PMI June 53.3% (−0.7pp vs May 54.0) — 20th straight month of expansion
ISM / PRNewswire · 2026-07-01
Manufacturing PMI eased to 53.3 (corresponds to ~2% real GDP annualized). New Orders 56.0 (−0.8pp, 6th straight month of expansion); Production 52.2 (−2.1pp). Demand sentiment positive (2.7-to-1 positive/negative comment ratio). Computer & Electronic Products among the four largest industries reporting increased new orders.
