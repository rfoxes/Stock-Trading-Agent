US-Iran / Strait of Hormuz: sixth consecutive day of strikes
CNN / NPR / Reuters · 2026-07-16
The US and Iran traded fire for a sixth straight day. US strikes targeted Iranian command centers and missile sites; Iran targeted US military bases in Gulf countries. The US struck an empty oil tanker bound for Kharg Island after reimposing a naval blockade on Iranian ports. Iran warned the Strait of Hormuz is an "unbreakable red line." Roughly 20% of the world's oil & gas transits Hormuz; the deadlock has disrupted trade and lifted fuel prices. The July 8 interim truce is in tatters with no path to a deal.
Market read-through: oil and yields up early, but NO >2% equity-futures gap
CNBC · 2026-07-16
Rising oil and Treasury yields were an early drag, contributing to the risk-off tone alongside chips. But equities did not gap: S&P 500 -0.51%, Nasdaq -1.47%, Dow -0.2%. The escalation is a live energy/inflation tail but did NOT trip the >2%-futures-gap HALT criterion. Both June inflation prints (CPI/PPI) predate this oil spike and understate forward energy pressure.
