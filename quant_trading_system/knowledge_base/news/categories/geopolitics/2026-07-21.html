US-Iran / Strait of Hormuz — the July conflict has resumed; ceasefire is on-again/off-again.
House of Commons Library / Britannica / Wikipedia · 2026-07-21
The April 7–8 ceasefire (and a June 14 memorandum meant to formally end the conflict within 60 days) broke down: in July the conflict resumed after Iran struck three commercial vessels that bypassed its preapproved transit route. Negotiations continue over a new Hormuz transit regime — Iran wants transit charges/tolls; Oman and the US oppose tolls. Markets are treating it as an "on-again/off-again" backdrop rather than a fresh shock.
