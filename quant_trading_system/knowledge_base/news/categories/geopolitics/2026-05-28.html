US-Iran negotiators reach tentative 60-day ceasefire MoU; Trump approval still pending
The Hill / CNN · 2026-05-28
Negotiators agreed to a 60-day Memorandum of Understanding extending the ceasefire and starting talks on Iran's nuclear program. Iran will not impose tolls on commercial ships transiting Hormuz and will begin demining. Trump must still approve and said the administration is "not satisfied" with the terms — risk of pullback is non-zero. Treasury Secretary Bessent said outcome depends on Trump.
