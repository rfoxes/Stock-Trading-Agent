Houthis struck two Saudi oil tankers in the Red Sea — the blockade turned kinetic.
Washington Post / Bloomberg / The National · 2026-07-23
Iran-backed Houthi militants said they fired missiles and drones at two Saudi tankers (Encelia and Layla) to enforce the naval blockade of Saudi ports declared earlier this week, causing a fire aboard the Encelia; crews reported safe. This is a material escalation from yesterday's announced-but-unenforced blockade — actual attacks on vessels now. Brent jumped +6.45% to ~$101.10 (first >$100 since May; ~40% MTD). Ships are diverting; Goldman sees Brent $120+ by Q4 if disruptions persist.
