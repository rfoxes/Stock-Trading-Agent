US issues 60-day waiver authorizing Iranian oil sales (carry-forward RESOLVED)
Treasury / NPR / Washington Examiner · 2026-06-23
Treasury Sec. Bessent announced a temporary 60-day general license authorizing the production, delivery and sale of Iranian oil, in exchange for Iran maintaining free transit through the Strait of Hormuz and admitting IAEA inspectors. This operationalizes the preliminary US-Iran agreement Trump signed June 17 (which ended the conflict begun Feb 28). Net oil-supply-positive (bearish crude) — Iran can re-access the global market (~$8B in revenue over two months estimated). Resolves the carried-forward "signed-or-slipped" question: the framework is now live.
