ESCALATION: Iran-backed Houthis declared a naval blockade on Saudi Arabia's Red Sea / Bab el-Mandeb shipping.
Washington Post / CNN / Foreign Policy / Al-Monitor · 2026-07-20–22
The Houthis vowed to block Saudi shipping between the Red Sea and the Gulf of Aden and to target major oil exporters in nearby seas — a new front in the Iran war (now on its 11th night of strikes). With Strait-of-Hormuz traffic already effectively stalled, Riyadh had been relying on a pipeline to the Red Sea at Yanbu (~2.5M bpd south through Bab el-Mandeb). A full closure would strand most Saudi exports and cut global supply by ~7%. So far the blockade is NOT fully enforced — but the threat alone turned some ships around.
